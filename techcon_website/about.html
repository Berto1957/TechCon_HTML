Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of passionate developers and tech leaders. Over the years, it has grown into a global movement attracting thousands of attendees annually.

Our Mission

The mission of TechCon is to inspire innovation, foster collaboration, and drive positive change through technology. We believe in the power of diverse voices and ideas to shape the future.
